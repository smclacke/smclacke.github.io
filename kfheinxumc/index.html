About

    The K.F. Hein Fund annually assigns one HKU exam candidate for the Art Participation Project. In collaboration with the UMC Utrecht, I was selected to make a new artwork for the educational building (A.A. Hijmans van den Berghgebouw) from the UMC. The students and/ or employees of UMC Utrecht were actively involved in the development of the work.
